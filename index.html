Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking your studio or portrait session at least 2-4 weeks in advance. For weddings and major corporate events, we advise booking 3-6 months beforehand to guarantee photographer availability.

Standard edits are delivered via our premium online gallery within 10 to 14 business days. Wedding and cinematic video packages can take between 4 to 6 weeks, as they undergo a thorough, high-end color grading and sorting phase.

Raw files represent an unfinished product. Under standard packages, we only supply professionally curated and edited images. However, our Luxury package includes the complete set of high-resolution RAW images for professional archiving.
